Introduction


“There are probably as many reasons as there are people to explain why so many who are well informed procrastinate” - Edward A. Charlesworth

A hobby is an enjoyable activity that a person chooses to do. One may consider hobbies to be actions such as reading, writing, gaming, and playing music. These are important because they give individuals pleasure in their free time. Students at the University of Rhode Island, especially first year students transitioning from highschool to college, may feel overwhelmed with the college workload. It is important for both their mental health and physical health that they are not too stressed. Hobbies can reduce the amount of stress people have. Although it is possible to allow hobbies to distract one from remaining on task with work, hobbies still play an essential role in our well being.

Methods


I created a google form with multiple questions regarding hobbies, stress, and the direct relationship between them. Google forms are anonymous and store the data so that it can be accessed to the person who created it.

I then shared the link to the form on my social media. I am aware that my sample group was mostly limited to my friends, however, most of my friends are college students and I asked others to share it as well so that I can get the best possible data from the google form.




Findings


All students in this sample group agreed that college made them feel stressed.
